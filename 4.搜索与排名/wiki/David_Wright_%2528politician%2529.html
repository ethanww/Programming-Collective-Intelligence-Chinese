David Wright (politician)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

David Wright (born December 22, 1966, Oakengates, Telford) is a politician in the United Kingdom, and Labour Party member of Parliament for Telford. He has been an MP since 2001.

Wright was a Town Councillor in Oakengates 1989-2000 and a Wrekin District Councillor from 1989-1997.
