KAZI is an Urban Contemporary and Community Radio station in Austin, Texas.

KAZI went on the air in August 1982 as a Community Radio station in Austin serving the African American community of the city, plus surrounding towns in the coverage area. The station operates on an Urban Contemporary format and plays all styles of music: R&B, Hip Hop, Gospel, Blues, Soul Reggae and Jazz.
