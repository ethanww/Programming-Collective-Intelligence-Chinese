Timothy Shell is an American internet entrepreneur. He is the CEO of Bomis. Shell has been with the company since its inception in 1996.

[edit]

Education

Shell has a high school diploma and he attended colleges classes in computer science until hiring on with Bomis.

[edit]

Other activities

Shell serves as the Executive Secretary on the Board of Trustees of the Wikimedia Foundation, but as of 2006 he makes very few new editorial contributions to Wikipedia.

Shell has lived in Chicago, Florida, and San Diego, and currently resides in Las Vegas.

Shell is an epicurian and a connoisseur of beer.
