May 2001

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

May2001 : January - February - March - April - May - June - July - August - September - October - November - December

[edit]

Events

  • May 1 - Chandra Levy disappears while jogging.
  • May 29 - Barbara and Jenna Bush prosecuted for under-age drinking after posing as over-21s in a Texas drinking establishment.
  • Eco-terrorists set near simultaneous arson fires at the University of Washington and at a poplar farm in Clatskanie, Ore.
  • In Afghanistan, the ruling Taliban militia announces a law requiring Hindus to wear identity labels to distinguish them from Muslims. The measure also requires Hindu women to be veiled for the first time.
[edit]

Deaths

  • May 11 - Douglas Adams, author of the cult science-fiction comedy The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, dies at the age of 49.
  • May 12 - Perry Como, singer, dies at the age of 89.
[edit]

Software

  • SGI releases XFS under an open source licence.
