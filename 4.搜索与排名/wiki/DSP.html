DSP

Jump to: navigation, search

The term "DSP", when used by itself, can refer to:

[edit]

Computing

  • The science and engineering discipline of digital signal processing.
  • The microprocessor class of digital signal processors.
  • Dylan Server Pages is a template engine for providing dynamic web site content using the Dylan language.
[edit]

Politics

  • The Democratic Left Party (Turkey) (Demokratik Sol Parti)
  • The German State Party (Deutsche Staatspartei), a minor political party of the Weimar Republic period.
  • One of several (unrelated) political parties called the Democratic Socialist Party:
    • Democratic Socialist Party (Australia), now the Democratic Socialist Perspective
    • Democratic Socialist Party (Japan)
    • Democratic Socialist Party (Prabodh Chandra) (India)
[edit]

Other

  • The sterilisation process Dry Sterilisation Process for the cold and fast sterilisation of surfaces.
  • The U.S. Air Force Defense Support Program early-warning satellites.
  • Deputy Superintendent in the Singapore Police Force
  • The Deutscher Spiele Preis, a board games award
  • In the form dsp or d.s.p. this abbreviation can stand for the Latin phrase decessit sine prole ("died without issue"). This often appears in genealogical tree-diagrams.
  • dsp, an experimental electronic music collective.
