Big Blue (disambiguation)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

The term Big Blue may have several meanings, including:

  • Big Blue, a common nickname for IBM
    • Big Blue Disk, a magazine deriving its name from the nickname
    • Nihon IBM Big Blue, a rugby team sponsored by the company
  • The Big Blue, a 1988 film directed by Luc Besson
  • Big Blue Machine, a group of advisors to the Ontario Progressive Conservative Party in the 1970s and 80s
  • Big Blue, a nickname for the New York Giants football team
  • The Big Blues, the 1962 debut album of Albert King
  • Big Blue River in Kansas, a tributary of the Kansas River
  • Big Blue River in Indiana, a tributary of the Driftwood River
  • Big Blue Bus, a municipal bus operator in West Los Angeles
  • Big Blue was the name used to refer to a crane that collapsed during the construction of Miller Park, killing three.
  • The Big Blue Bug, a giant termite mascot of New England Pest Control
  • Big Blue, an area in the F-Zero series and Super Smash Bros. Melee video games
  • Big Blue, a nickname for a large type of American handball
