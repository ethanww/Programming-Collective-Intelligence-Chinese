University of Colorado System

(Redirected from University of Colorado)

The University of Colorado ("CU") System consists of three campuses:

  • University of Colorado at Boulder (flagship campus)
  • University of Colorado at Colorado Springs
  • University of Colorado at Denver and Health Sciences Center, created by the merger of the University of Colorado Health Sciences Center and University of Colorado at Denver

The University of Colorado System is governed by an elected, nine-member Board of Regents of the University of Colorado.
