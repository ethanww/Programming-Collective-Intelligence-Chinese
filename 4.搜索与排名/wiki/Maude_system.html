Maude system

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

The Maude system is an implementation of rewriting logic developed at SRI International. It is similar in its general approach to Joseph Goguen's OBJ3 implementation of equational logic, but based on rewriting logic rather than order-sorted equational logic, and with a heavy emphasis on powerful meta-programming based on reflection.
