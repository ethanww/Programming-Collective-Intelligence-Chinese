Walter Werzowa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

Walter Thomas Werzowa (born in Vienna 15 December 1960) is a composer and member of the 1980s Austrian sampling band Edelweiss. He is founder of the 'sound design' company Musikvergnuegen (which translates as 'Love of Music' from the German language).

Whilst Werzowa is known for being the composer of music for films such as Taking Lives, Mimic 2, Hellraiser: Inferno and Cherry Falls.

His best known piece of music is in fact the short Intel jingle that once accompanied the firm's advertising.
