The Indonesian collaboration article will be voted each week. The goal of this department is to improve Indonesian-related Indonesian underdeveloped articles. However, it is very possible that a more established article is chosen, preferably until it reaches the featured article status. Every week, new nominees are added and voters can participate to choose the suitable article.

The current Indonesian collaboration is Religion in Indonesia.

Please read the project page and share your thought.
You can help by improving the article or discuss it on the collaboration discussion page.

[edit]

How it works

Being an Indonesian is not a requirement to contribute in this project. The voting process is generated when an article is being improved. Therefore, there will be two processes, article improvement and voting. In addition, a new article nomination winner should have been decided just before the current collaboration finishes.

[edit]

Nominate an article

Please follow the steps below

  1. Copy this template.
  2. Paste it at the bottom of the nominations section.
  3. Change the entries of the template with relevant article name, nomination date (with "month date, year" format).
  4. Put your comments on why do you think we should improve this article.
  5. Copy the {{Indonesian collaboration nominee}} template on the article's discussion's page.
  6. Each time you vote, please update the votes tally for the article.
  7. Start voting. Don't forget to put your signature!

[edit]

Overall procedure

  1. Think a suitable article for this collaboration. Try to choose a less-developed article, although you can choose any article.
  2. Nominate the article for the collaboration.
  3. Don't feel bad about someone because the article you've nominated has lost or losing. This is our project, we work together. You can always add another nominee the week after.
  4. Wait until the whole procedure of nominating an article has done.
  5. Change the {{Indonesian collaboration nominee}} template with {{Current Indonesia collaboration}} template on the article's discussion page.
  6. Start improving the article the best you can!
  7. If you want, please discuss any relevant issue with other editors here.
  8. When the current article's improvement time has finished, change the {{Current Indonesia collaboration}} template with {{Past Indonesia collaboration|Date=monthname date|Year=year}} template.
  9. Repeat the same pattern :D
[edit]

Voting

A vote or a show of support for an article shows your commitment to support and aid in collaborating on that specific article if it is chosen. Although you are not required to fulfill that commitment, we ask that you only support articles that you are able to contribute to so that this collaboration's goals of expanding and improving articles can adequately be achieved. Please add only support votes. Opposing votes will not affect the result, as the winner is simply the one with the most support votes.
