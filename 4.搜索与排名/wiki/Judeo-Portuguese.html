Judeo-Portuguese

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

Judeo-Portuguese is the generally extinct Jewish language of the Jews of Portugal.

As Portuguese Jews mixed with other expelled Sephardim, it influenced the Judeo-Spanish or Ladino language. It is also an influence for Papiamento and Saramaccan.
