Kinetics, a subsidiary of NCR Corporation, is a technology company specializing in self-service software and hardware for the travel industry. Among its products is the award-winning Virgin Blue online check-in service.

[edit]

History

The company was founded in 1988 by David Melnik and acquired by NCR Corporation in 2004.

[edit]

Industry

  • Kinetics, along with ARINC, IBM, IER, Materna, ServiceTec, SITA and Unisys, is a part of the IATA Common Use Self Service (CUSS) Management Group.
[edit]

Management

  • President: Hervé Muller (March 2006 - present)
  • CEO: David Melnik (1988 - 2005)
