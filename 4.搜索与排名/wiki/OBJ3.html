OBJ3

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

OBJ3 is a version of OBJ based on order-sorted rewriting. OBJ3 is agent-oriented and runs on AKCL. It is now of (important) historical interest since newer versions of the OBJ family are available.

Reference

  • Introducing OBJ3, Joseph Goguen et al., SRI-CSL-88-9, SRI International, USA, 1988.
