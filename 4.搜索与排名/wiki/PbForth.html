PbForth

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search
The correct title of this article is pbForth. The initial letter is capitalized due to technical restrictions.

pbFORTH is a programming language developed for Lego Mindstorms. It is based on the stack-oriented Forth language, and was developed by Ralph Hempel.
