OutSystems offers an agile software platform to build, change and operate enterprise applications that integrate with existing systems and reach users via web browsers, email and mobile interfaces. Additionally the company provides a wide set of open source applications that can be quickly tailored to meet each company's specific needs, further accelerating the delivery of high impact business solutions.

[edit]

History

OutSystems is a multinational software company created in March 2001.

The company closed a 1 million Euros round of seed funding in October 2001 with the Dutch Venture Capitalist firm NesBIC CTe FUND (Fortis Group). In February 2005 OutSystems closed a â‚¬ 2.2 million Series B round of investment led by PME Investimentos.

Since its creation OutSystems has been achieving yearly growths of around 100%, consistently expanding its international customer base.

In 2003 OutSystems was identified by Fortune Magazine as one of the 6 most promising startups. Market analysts like Gartner, Butler and OVUM have identified OutSystems as an innovative software company.

OutSystems sells directly in the USA, UK, Netherlands, Spain and Portugal and has global partnerships with top technology firms to target other geographies.
