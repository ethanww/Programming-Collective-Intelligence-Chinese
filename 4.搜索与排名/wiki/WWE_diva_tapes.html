[edit]

History

WWE has been making DVD's and Video's of the women of the WWE since 1999. The first Diva wrestling video to come out was, Come get some: the women of the WWF. Since then the WWE have continued to produce WWE diva videos to this day.

[edit]

1999

The first Diva video released

The WWE video was released May, 1999. Only Debra, Chyna and Sable made the cover on the US version of the video and Debra, Chyna & Tori made the UK version, all the rest of the divas such as Terri, Jackie & Luna made the back cover.

[edit]

2000

The second Diva video was released June, 2000 which included Trish Stratus, The Kat, Terri Runnels, Tori and Ivory, all Divas made the cover

[edit]

2001

The Third video & DVD was released May, 2001 Which included Trish Stratus, Debra Marshall, Chyna, Lita, Tori, The Kat all Divas made the front cover to this as well.

[edit]

2002

The Fourth Video to be made was released July, 2002 Which included every diva in the wwe at the time but only Torrie Wilson made the cover.
