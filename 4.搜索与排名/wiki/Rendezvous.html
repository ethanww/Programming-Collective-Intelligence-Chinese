Rendezvous is the anglification of the French word Rendez-vous meaning "appointment". (literally, meet you); it is pronounced RAWN-day-voo. In a general sense, it means any type of meeting or generally between two persons. It can also be a place either for a meeting at an agreed time, or where people often meet.

  • Rendezvous is the short name for Virtual Rendezvous, both an open software project and the company leading it.
  • Rendez-Vous, the title of a 1986 album by the French artist Jean Michel Jarre
  • rendezvous, an orbital maneuver where the two arrive at the same orbit, make the orbital velocities the same, and bring them together
  • Rendezvous (plan 9), a system call of Plan 9
  • Rendezvous problem, a class of computational problems
  • Buick Rendezvous, the name of an automobile manufactured by General Motors Corporation
  • C'était un rendez-vous, a short film made in 1976 by Claude Lelouch
  • Rendezvous (movie), a 1935 film
  • Rendezvous is a short name for TIBCO Rendezvous, a software product.
  • Rendezvous (whitehorse), an event in Whitehorse, Yukon that occurs in February
  • "Rendezvous", the name of a song by a British progressive house act Tilt featuring German DJ Paul Van Dyk
  • Rendezvous, the name of the cultural festival of the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi.
  • Rendezvous (album), an album made by singer/songwriter Christopher Cross in 1992
  • Rendezvous, a language construct in the programming language Ada
  • Rendezvous, now called Bonjour, a computer network technology developed by Apple Computer.
  • Rendezvous at the Mountain, is the title of the last level of the computer game Lemmings.
  • Rendezvous Entertainment is a contenporary jazz label which co-founded by saxphonist Dave Koz, Frank Cody and Hyman Katz in 2002.
  • For a rendezvous in space, see Space rendezvous.
