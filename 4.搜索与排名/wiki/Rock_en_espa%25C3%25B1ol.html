Spanish language rock and roll

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

(Redirected from Rock en español)
Jump to: navigation, search

Spanish language rock and roll (Spanish: Rock en Español) borrows heavily from American, British, Irish, and Australian rock and roll music and from popular music of Spanish-speaking cultures (cumbia, ranchera, rumba, tango, etc) and has evolved from a cult-like music movement to a more well established music genre.

Today, rock en español includes bands from all over Latin America, France, Spain, and the United States:
