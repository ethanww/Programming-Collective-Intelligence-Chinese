XL or xl may stand for:

  • a clothing size that generally means Extra Large. European clothing-size standard EN 13402 defines the size code XL to refer to chest girths 110–118 cm for men's clothes and bust girths 107–119 cm for women's clothes. Manufacturers also use their own non-standard defining body measurements for this size code.
  • forty (XL), the Roman numeral
  • The XL series of computers in the Atari 8-bit family (including the 600XL, 800XL and 1200XL)
  • XL Recordings
  • XL (magazine), a pornographic magazine for fat admirers
  • XL Programming Language
  • A brand name of Excel Airways, XL.com
  • Super Bowl XL, the fortieth Super Bowl
  • Weather STAR XL, a cable system from The Weather Channel
  • Winbook XL, a laptop computer
  • XL Capital Ltd. an insurance and reinsurance company headquartered in Bermuda.
