New Hampshire gubernatorial election, 2006

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search
This article or section contains information about an upcoming or ongoing election.
Content may change dramatically as the election approaches and unfolds.

The 2006 New Hampshire gubernatorial election will take place on November 7, 2006. Incumbent John Lynch is expected to run for re-election. In a SurveyUSA poll released Feb. 16, 2006, Lynch's approval rating was 70% [1]. A recent University of New Hampshire poll showed 67% for Lynch and only 12% for James B. Coburn, who is largely unknown statewide.
