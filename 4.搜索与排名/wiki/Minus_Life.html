Minus Life

A five-piece band from Brisbane Australia.

They identify themselves as "modern Death Metal", but could arguable fall into many other genres and do not have the full grating impact of most "death" metal.

Their most frequented venue is HMB a.k.a. "the Basement" downstairs from the Hilton Hotel in the Queen Street Mall, but recently have been playing further from home.


[edit]

Line-up

Scott Moss - vocals

Ryan O'Connell - guitar

Matt Bellette - keyboards

Kent Haines - bass

Travis Henderson - drums

[edit]

Releases

2 demo disks both in 2002

Eternal Urban Megacide (EUM) 2004

and a new album pending


Some Sample tracks from EUM are available from their web-site free of charge.
