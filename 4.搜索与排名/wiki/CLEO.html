Cleo

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

Cleo is a female given name that is short for Cleopatra and an alternate spelling for Clio.

  • People who are commonly referred to solely by their given name Cleo include:
    • Cleo, Of vocal group Cleopatra (band)
    • Miss Cleo, a self-proclaimed psychic.
  • Things named Cleo include:
    • Hurricane Cleo, a hurricane that hit the Caribbean and Florida in 1964.
    • Cleo, an Australian women's magazine.
    • Cleo, a Swedish comedy TV series broadcast in two seasons 2002 and 2003.
    • Letters to Cleo, an alternative rock band from Boston, Massachusetts
  • CLEO, a collaboration amongst physicists, based at the Cornell Laboratory of Elementary Particle Physics
    • CLEO is also the name of a particle detector operated by the same collaboration of physicists
  • CLEO, the Cisco router in low Earth orbit, is a satellite payload extending the Internet into space
  • CLEO (Clear Language for Expressing Orders), the programming language for LEO computers
  • Cleo is a fish in the 1940 movie Pinocchio
  • Cleo is a basset hound dog in the Jackie Cooper's 1950s TV show The People's Choice
  • CLEO is an abbreviation for "Conference on Laser and Electro-Optics"
  • CLEO is an abbreviation for Chief Law Enforcement Officer, typically the Sheriff or Chief of Police in most American localities.
  • Cleo is a fashion store located in Elnesvågen, Norway.
