The Cambridge University Robot Language (CURL) is an interactive robot control environment which was developed at the Department of Engineering, University of Cambridge. The CURL environment facilitates both direct control and task-level control of a robot arm and has been specifically designed for applications in rehabilitation. It provides an intuitive method of performing pick and place operations through the definition of objects and goals within a world model.
