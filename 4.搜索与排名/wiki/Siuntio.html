Siuntio (IPA: /ˈsiuntio/), or Sjundeå in Swedish is a municipality of Finland.

It is located in the province of Southern Finland and is part of the Uusimaa region. The municipality has a population of 5,312 (2004-12-31) and covers an area of 248.18 km² of which 6.63 km² is water. The population density is 22.0 inhabitants per km².

The municipality is bilingual with the majority being Finnish and the minority Swedish-speakers.
