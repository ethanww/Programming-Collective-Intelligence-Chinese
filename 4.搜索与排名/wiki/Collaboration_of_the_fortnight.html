Anyone is free to contribute, but please don't nominate or support an option unless you are willing to help it improve if it is elected as the fortnight's collaboration. Only positive support votes will be counted, except in the case of a tie. Please also only vote for a collaboration if you are willing to take part.

[edit]

Purpose of the collaboration

Personally, I think the purpose of the collaboration should be to work up a rugby union-related article to featured article status. The articles should either be in a key rugby area (eg pages on the domestic competitions) or alternatively close enough to featured article status already such that a collaboration is likely to bring it up to FA quality, --HamedogTalk|@ 02:04, 25 February 2006 (UTC)

[edit]

North America 4 (NA4)

Nominated on 30 August 2006.

Comments

  • I would like to nominate the North America 4 tournament. It was previously mentioned to nominate one particular club team in the US to raise interest in North America, however this tournament features two teams from the US and Canada respectively. These teams are composed of the best domestic players available. The IRB is pumping a lot of money into it, with the hopes of bringing professional rugby to North America. In fact the exposure of this tournament has led to some players representing their country and others getting professional contracts.Am rockstar 17:06, 31 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Sure, I think it's a great idea. There has recently been an influx in creating North American rugby articles, and I think the article would do well to have some new sets of eyes on it hoopydinkConas tá tú? 18:35, 31 August 2006 (UTC)
  • I support this nomination. The current article is very small, but can very much be expanded. COTF will be great for this article.Cvene64 22:44, 31 August 2006 (UTC)
  • Support, IRB is getting stale.--HamedogTalk|@ 12:08, 3 September 2006 (UTC)
