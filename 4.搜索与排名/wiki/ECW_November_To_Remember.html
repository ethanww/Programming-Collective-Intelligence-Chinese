ECW November To Remember

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

November To Remember (normally abbreviated to N2R) was an Eastern Championship Wrestling and Extreme Championship Wrestling show (and, later, pay per view) held annually during the month of November between 1993 and 2000. The event was considered to be ECW's flagship PPV.

Eastern Championship Wrestling

[edit]

1993

November to Remember 1993 took place on November 13, 1993 from the ECW Arena in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

  • Salvatore Bellomo defeated Rockin' Rebel by forfeit in a Chair match
  • The Sandman fought Jim Neidhart to a no-contest
  • The Public Enemey (Rocco Rock and Johnny Grunge) defeated Badd Company (Pat Tanaka and Paul Diamond)
  • Kevin Sullivan defeated Tommy Cairo
  • Malia Hosaka defeated Sherri Martel by disqualification
  • The Suicide Blondes (Johnny Hotbody and Tony Stetson) defeated the Bad Breed (Ian and Axl Rotten) to retain the ECW Tag Team Championship
  • Johnny Gunn and Tommy Dreamer defeated The Suicide Blondes (Johnny Hotbody and Tony Stetson) to win the ECW Tag Team Championship
  • The Tazmaniac defeated Tommy Dreamer
  • Mr. Hughes defeated Johnny Gunn
  • Sabu and Road Warrior Hawk defeated Terry Funk and King Kong Bundy
    • Sabu pinned Funk to win Funk's ECW World Television Championship.

Extreme Championship Wrestling

[edit]

1994

November to Remember 1994 took place on November 5, 1994 from the ECW Arena in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

  • J.T. Smith defeated Hack Meyers
  • The Pitbulls (Pitbull #1 and Pitbull #2) defeated the Bad Breed (Axl and Ian Rotten)
  • 2 Cold Scorpio defeated Mr. Hughes
  • Tommy Dreamer defeated Tommy Cairo
  • Dean Malenko defeated The Tazmaniac
  • Shane Douglas defeated Ron Simmons to retain the ECW World Television Championship
  • The Public Enemy (Rocco Rock and Johnny Grunge) defeated Cactus Jack and Mikey Whipwreck to win the ECW Tag Team Championship
  • Chris Benoit fought Sabu to a no-contest
    • The match was stopped after Sabu broke his neck after a backdrop.
  • Chris Benoit fought 2 Cold Scorpio to a double count-out.
[edit]

1995

November to Remember 1995 took place on November 18, 1995 from the ECW Arena in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

  • Don E. Allen fought Broad Street Bully to a no-contest
    • Buh Buh Ray Dudley pinned both men while acting as a ring announcer
  • Konnan defeated Jason Knight with Taz as Special Guest Referee
  • Stevie Richards (w/The Blue Meanie) defeated Pablo Marquez
  • The Pitbulls (Pitbull #1 and Pitbull #2) (w/Francine) defeated The Eliminators (John Kronus and Perry Saturn) (w/Jason Knight)
  • Rey Misterio, Jr. defeated Psicosis in a Mexican Death match
  • The Sandman and 2 Cold Scorpio defeated The Public Enemy (Rocco Rock and Johnny Grunge) to retain the ECW Tag Team Championship
  • Bill Alfonso defeated Tod Gordon with Beulah McGillicutty as Special Guest Referee
  • The Sandman fought ECW Heavyweight Champion Mikey Whipwreck to a no-contest
    • The Sandman was attacked by Steve Austin, Whipreck retains the ECW World Heavyweight Championship.
  • Mikey Whipwreck defeated Steve Austin to retain the ECW World Heavyweight Championship
  • Sabu defeated Hack Meyers
  • Terry Funk and Tommy Dreamer defeated Raven and Cactus Jack

November To Remember 1997 took place on November 30, 1997 from the Golden Dome in Monaca, Pennsylvania. It was the first NTR to take place on Pay-per-view.

  • Pre-show match: Chris Chetti and Spike Dudley defeated Erin O'Grady and Paul Diamond
    • Dudley pinned O'Grady.
  • Chris Candido and Lance Storm defeated Tommy Rogers and Jerry Lynn(16:42)
    • Candido pinned Rogers.
    • This started out as Candido vs. Rogers.
  • Mikey Whipwreck defeated Justin Credible (w/Jason)(7:15)
    • Whipreck pinned Credible after a Whippersnapper.
  • Taz defeated Pitbull #2 (w/Pitbull #1 and Lance Wright) to retain the ECW World Television Championship(1:29)
    • Taz forced Pitbull #1 to submit.
  • The F.B.I. (Tracy Smothers and Little Guido) (w/Tommy Rich) defeated The Dudley Boyz (Buh Buh Ray and D-Von) (w/Big Dick Dudley, Sign Guy Dudley, and Joel Gertner), The Hardcore Chair Swingin' Freaks (Balls Mahoney and Axl Rotten) and The Gangstanators (New Jack and John Kronus) in a Four-Way Dance to retain the ECW Tag Team Championship(14:32)
    • Buh Buh pinned Kronus(10:04)
    • Rotten pinned D-Von(12:27)
    • Guido pinned Mahoney(14:32)
  • Rob Van Dam (w/Bill Alfonso) fought Tommy Dreamer (w/Beulah McGillicutty) to a no-contest in a Flag match(16:02)
  • Sabu (w/Bill Alfonso) defeated The Sandman in a Tables and Ladder match(20:55)
    • Sabu pinned Sandman after an Atomic Arabian Facebuster with the ladder.
  • Shane Douglas (w/Francine) defeated Bam Bam Bigelow to win the ECW World Heavyweight Championship(25:02)
    • Douglas pinned Bigelow after a Belly to Belly Suplex.

November To Remember 1998 took place on November 1, 1998 from the Lakefront Arena in New Orleans, Louisiana.

  • Tommy Rogers (w/Chris Chetti) defeated Tracy Smothers (w/Tommy Rich and Little Guido)(7:51)

  • Lance Storm (w/Tammy Lynn Bytch) defeated Jerry Lynn (w/Tammy Lynn Sytch and Mikey Whipwreck as Special Guest Referees)(16:48)
    • Storm pinned Lynn.
  • Masato Tanaka and Balls Mahoney (w/Axl Rotten) defeated The Dudley Boyz (Buh Buh Ray and D-Von) (w/Joel Gertner and Sign Guy Dudley) to win the ECW Tag Team Championship(15:01)
    • Balls pinned D-Von and Tanaka pinned Buh Buh.
  • Jake Roberts and Tommy Dreamer defeated Justin Credible and Jack Victory(12:26)
    • Dreamer and Roberts simultaneously pinned Credible.
  • Sabu, Rob Van Dam and Taz defeated The Triple Threat (Shane Douglas, Bam Bam Bigelow and Chris Candido)(12:57)
    • Sabu pinned Douglas.

November To Remember 1999 took place on November 7, 1999 from the Burt Flickinger Center in Buffalo, New York.

  • Da Baldies (Spanish Angel, Tony DeVito, Vito LoGrasso, and P.N. News) defeated New Jack and The Hardcore Chair Swingin' Freaks (Axl Rotten and Balls Mahoney) in a Handicap match(8:21)

  • Mike Awesome defeated Masato Tanaka to retain the ECW World Heavyweight Championship(12:26)
    • Awesome pinned Tanaka.
  • Rob Van Dam defeated Taz to retain the ECW World Television Championship(14:34)
    • Van Dam pinned Taz.
  • Rhino and The Impact Players (Justin Credible and Lance Storm) (w/Dawn Marie) defeated Raven, Tommy Dreamer, and The Sandman (w/Francine)(9:19)
    • Credible pinned Sandman.

November To Remember 2000 took place on November 5, 2000 from the Odeum Expo Center in Villa Park, Illinois.

  • Simon Diamond and Johnny Swinger defeated Christian York and Joey Matthews(5:20)
    • Swinger pinned York after C.W. Anderson interfered and hit York with a Anderson Spinebuster.
  • Kid Kash defeated C.W. Anderson(10:45)
    • Kash pinned Anderson after a Tornado DDT.
  • Amish Roadkill, Danny Doring, and Spike Dudley defeated Hot Commodity (Chris Hamrick, Julio Dinero, and E.Z. Money)(8:23)
    • Dudley pinned Hamrick after a Acid Drop.
  • Nova defeated Chris Chetti in a Loser Leaves Town match(9:43)
    • Nova pinned Chetti after a Kryptonite Krunch from the top rope.
  • Balls Mahoney and Chilly Willy defeated Da Baldies (Angel and Tony DeVito) in a Flaming Tables match(12:11)
    • Mahoney slammed DeVito through a table to win the match.
  • Rhino defeated New Jack to retain the ECW World Television Championship(7:55)
    • Rhino pinned Jack after a Gore through a table.
  • The F.B.I. (Little Guido and Tony Mamaluke) (w/Sal E. Graziano) defeated The Unholy Alliance (Yoshihiro Tajiri and Mikey Whipwreck) to retain the ECW Tag Team Championship(15:46)
    • Guido pinned Tajiri after a Double Bulldog.
    • During the match, Whipwreck was injured and replaced by Super Crazy.
  • Steve Corino (w/Jack Victory and Dawn Marie) defeated Justin Credible (w/Francine), Jerry Lynn (c) and The Sandman in a Double Jeopardy match to win the ECW World Heavyweight Championship(24:07)
    • Credible pinned Lynn after a That's Incredible! and Corino pinned Sandman after a Russian Leg Sweep with a singapore cane(16:21)
    • Corino pinned Credible after a Old School Expulsion(24:07)
    • The rules of this match was that two matches were happening at the same time, Corino vs. Sandman and Credible vs. Lynn, and the winners would continue the match against each other while the losers remained at ringside.
