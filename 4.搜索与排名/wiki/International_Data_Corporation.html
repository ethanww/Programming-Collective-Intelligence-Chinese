International Data Corporation

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search
IDC "Analyze the Future"-logo.

International Data Corporation (IDC) is a market research and analysis firm specializing in information technology, telecommunications and consumer technology. IDC is a subsidiary of IDG. IDC is headquartered in Framingham, Massachusetts and claims to have over 850 analysts in 50 countries.[1]

Patrick McGovern founded IDC in 1964 after a UNIVAC executive complained to him about the lack of market research available on the emerging computer industry.[2]

[edit]

IDC companies

IDC has five subsidiary companies providing analysis and consultancy services for specific industries.

  • Energy Insights covers the energy industry
  • Health Industry Insights covers the healthcare industry
  • Financial Insights covers the financial services industry
  • Government Insights covers the public sector
  • Manufacturing Insights covers the manufacturing industry
